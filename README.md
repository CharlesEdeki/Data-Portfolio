### ✈️ Airline Delays & Cancellations - Performance Analysis
> *Power BI · DAX · Power Query · Aviation Analytics*

Across 1.9 million US domestic flights, fewer than 6 in 10 passengers experienced an on-time departure. This project breaks down why - by carrier (a 29-point performance gap between best and worst), by cancellation cause (weather vs. carrier-driven), and by day of week (Monday is structurally the worst day to fly, and the data explains exactly why).

### 📞 Call Centre Performance - Operational Analysis
> *Power BI · Excel · Operations Analytics · Python*

5,000 calls. 946 unanswered. 1,354 unresolved. The 3.40/5.0 average satisfaction score sounds manageable until you see that 1 in 5 customers is rating the experience a 1 or 2 — and that the miss rate holds constant regardless of call volume. This analysis finds the structural constraints, benchmarks every agent, and surfaces a counterintuitive finding: speed of answer has virtually zero correlation with customer satisfaction.

📂 [View Project →](https://github.com/CharlesEdeki/Call-Centre-Agent-Performance---Power-BI-Dashboard)

### 🚚 FedEx Delivery Process - Comprehensive Analysis
> *Power BI · Logistics Analytics · Python · Process Mapping*

A full analytical case study on 2,760 FedEx deliveries from Power BI dashboard through to BPMN process mapping, root cause analysis, and a structured recommendations framework. The core finding: stormy weather drives a 40.2% disruption rate vs. 13.4% in clear conditions, and the current process has no weather-contingency routing at all. A predictable failure, repeatedly absorbed.
